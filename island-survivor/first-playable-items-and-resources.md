# Extraction: Dead Isles — First Playable Items and Resources

## purpose
This document defines the first playable item set, resource ladder, survival consumables, building pieces, and early economy support.

## tools and weapons in first playable
Included:
- knife
- spear
- axe
- pickaxe
- bow
- machete
- pistol

## timing notes
### bow
The bow is craftable early on Home Island.

### pistol
The pistol becomes available through the engineer after mining progression involving coal and iron from Mining Island.

## durability
Tools and weapons should wear out slowly. Durability should matter, but should not become annoying micromanagement in the first playable.

## healing items
Included:
- bandage
- small medikit

## food and water
### food direction
- simple cooked foods
- lootable tins

### water direction
- refillable carried water bottles

## raw resources
Base resources in first playable:
- wood
- fiber
- stone
- scrap
- cloth
- rope
- coal
- iron ore
- animal hide
- infected residue
- gunpowder

## processed materials
Included:
- charcoal
- iron ingots
- planks
- nails
- arrows
- small pistol bullets

## refining and smelting
Refining should begin in the first playable because the ammunition path depends on it.

This means the first playable should already include:
- ore gathering
- charcoal production
- iron ingot production
- bullet crafting support

## first playable buildables
Included:
- campfire
- storage crate
- bedroll
- hut pieces
- walls
- door
- crafting bench
- furnace
- farm plot
- engineer's hut
- ammo bench

## electricity and solar
Electricity and solar systems should not be in the first playable. They should unlock later through Industrial Island progression.

## infected residue role
Infected residue acts as a currency-like progression resource used alongside real materials and quest progression.

It should support things like:
- specialist services
- item access through NPCs
- weapon unlock costs alongside real materials

### example
A pistol could require:
- engineer quest progress
- iron ingots
- gunpowder
- infected residue

## NPC economy direction
Rescued NPCs should function as traders and service providers rather than passive background characters.

## engineer reward direction
The engineer questline should unlock:
- mine access or key progression
- pistol access
- bullet crafting progression

The cleanest interpretation is staged:
1. engineer unlocks mine access first
2. player returns with mining progress
3. engineer unlocks pistol and bullet support

## progression summary
### early survival
- knife
- spear
- axe
- bow
- campfire
- storage crate
- bedroll
- simple home structures

### engineer progression
- rescue engineer
- build engineer hut
- support engineer
- unlock Mining Island

### mining progression
- gather coal
- gather iron ore
- use furnace
- create charcoal and iron ingots

### firearm progression
- unlock pistol
- unlock ammo bench
- craft small pistol bullets

## avoid
- too many firearm types too early
- advanced medicine complexity in first playable
- electricity in the opening progression
- random gun drops that bypass the intended engineer and mining loop
