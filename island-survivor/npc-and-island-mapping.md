# NPC and Island Mapping - Draft 1

Based on Wilko's current direction.

## Confirmed island order / role concept
### 1. Home Island
Starting area, first base, early survival, rescue of the mining engineer.

### 2. Mining Island
First destination island after home, unlocked through the mining engineer quest chain.
A hidden map or clue eventually points onward to the Luxury Hotel.

### 3. Luxury Hotel Island
Early-to-mid game civilian supply and story island.
Strong source of food and broad scavenging loot.

### 4. Industrial Storage Island
Industrial tools, batteries, solar gear, and utility progression.

### 5. Military Base Island
Human-threat-heavy island with advanced weapons progression and major story escalation.

### 6. Research Base Island
Hard late-game island focused on mutants, scientific equipment, chemicals, and the final emotional story beats.

## Story implications of current NPC placement
This structure creates a good emotional ramp:
- early rescue: practical survivor allies
- mid-game rescue: camp becomes more human and functional
- later rescue: personal stakes rise with discovery of your child
- final major story beat: reaching the partner at the research base

## Family-search structure
### Current family direction
- player is searching for a small family group
- first child found is unrelated
- later child found is the player's own child
- partner is found at the end, but dead

### Why this works well
This gives the game emotional progression without resolving the main personal goal too early.

The unrelated child provides:
- warmth
- camp life
- humour / distraction moments
- a sense that the player is rebuilding responsibility

Finding the player's own child later raises the emotional stakes and gives the rescue arc a stronger midpoint or late-midpoint payoff.

The partner being dead at the end makes the story bittersweet rather than neat and tidy.

## Character function ideas
### Mining Engineer
- unlocks first off-island progression
- gives mining-related quests
- explains why the mine matters

### Builder
- unlocks stronger structures
- enables stone upgrades for base building
- improves walls, huts, and defensive construction

### Medic
- unlocks better healing items, bandages, and treatment
- may also turn zombie/sample materials into useful medical outputs

### Technician
- unlocks power systems, batteries, solar, electronics, lighting, radios, utility stations

### Farmer
- unlocks food sustainability, farm plots, and renewable inputs

### Unrelated child
- adds camp personality
- can receive simple tasks
- may get distracted or wander off
- later behaves better once your own older child is found

### Gunsmith
- unlocks advanced gun crafting / repair / modification
- uses special materials plus service cost to create higher-end equipment

### Scientist
- unlocks research-driven crafting, advanced materials, outbreak knowledge, mutant-related progression

### Your child
- major emotional rescue point
- helps stabilise camp life and the unrelated child
- can contribute to small safe camp tasks depending on tone

### Partner
- final major emotional target
- found dead at the research base
- likely delivers final truth through notes, recordings, research logs, or final scene context

## Island-by-island progression feel
### Home Island
Purpose:
- base establishment
- primitive crafting
- early zombies
- rescue mining engineer

Completion feeling:
- camp is viable
- first key NPC rescued
- first off-island quest chain opened

### Mining Island
Purpose:
- obtain ore / progression materials
- rescue builder and medic
- start better base progression

Completion feeling:
- can improve tools / healing / structures
- hidden route toward hotel becomes available after enough engineer quests

### Luxury Hotel Island
Purpose:
- broad civilian supply runs
- food access
- story clues
- rescue technician, farmer, and unrelated child

Completion feeling:
- camp becomes more alive and sustainable
- power / farming / utility systems become possible

### Industrial Storage Island
Purpose:
- industrial tools and infrastructure
- unlock solar, batteries, heavy tools, better utility progression
- rescue gunsmith

Completion feeling:
- stronger base utility and ranged-combat progression

### Military Base Island
Purpose:
- major human-combat escalation
- advanced weapon access
- rescue scientist and your child

Completion feeling:
- personal stakes intensify sharply
- combat progression expands
- research path becomes clearer

### Research Base Island
Purpose:
- hardest island
- mutants / special infected
- scientific truth / outbreak answers
- final family payoff with partner

Completion feeling:
- emotional climax
- knowledge climax
- endgame transition

## Quest and unlocking logic
Current best-fit unlocking system:
- NPC quests
- maps
- clues found through progression

This is stronger than simple linear level unlocking because it:
- encourages revisits
- supports exploration
- makes progression feel earned
- fits the island-extraction structure naturally

## Current design strengths from this mapping
1. Each island now has both gameplay value and emotional value.
2. Camp progression and story progression are tightly linked.
3. NPC rescues naturally unlock new systems instead of arbitrary tech tiers.
4. The family arc has a clear emotional ladder: unrelated child -> your child -> dead partner.
5. The hotel sits in a strong role as a broad civilian supply and life-support island.
